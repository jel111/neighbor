What is Neighborsit All About?

What is it?

Neighbor Sit is a Web Site with companion app to bring back that sense of community. In the last few decades we have seen a shift away from the Neghborhoods where everyone knew and helped each other. In these days of a "Worldwide" community we have lost our neighborhoods that are physically right next door. Let's bring that back together!

  • Tell Me More

What are our goals?

Our Goals are to bring our communities back together again like it used to be. Like back in the day when people waved hi to each other and knew each others kids. When we could call a neighbor and not feel "Weird" to ask them to watch Johnny for an hour. We don't know them anymore to ask them taht question.

  • We Believe!

How can I join?

We make it very easy to join. Just add your email to our mailing list and we will keep you up to date on how we are progressing and when we will get up and running. We hope you will help us shape our future. We will have Contests and Giveaway's. Please spread the word and don't forget to sign up!

one neighbor at a time!

Why we think this is a good idea.

A great idea!

I do not know how many people i have talked to abiut Neighborsit and i have yet to find someone who says this is a bad idea. Sure, there are some that say it will be hard to do and we can't take on Facebook but we are not trying to take on FAcebook we are trying to make a much different app that helps us believe in community again. With all this technology we have an opportunity to have it work for us not just sell us stuff we do not need.
